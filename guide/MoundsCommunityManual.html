Welcome to the Community Mound Monitoring Module


What's this?

This is a field data collection app for Android 6.x and higher for monitoring the condition of burial mounds in your vicinity. This module allows you to record the dimensions, GPS points, and basic details of burial mound health and accompany them with pictures from on board camera. You can sync the information into an online server and retrieve it from there via email. The module is disseminated under CC-by license and has been designed by FAIMS.

How do I use this module?

  1. Login/Sign up: Ensure you have created user identity with true email (it is essential for data retrieval)

  2. Control: after login go to three dots in top right corner of the app and "Enable internal GPS"

  3. Control: Upon visiting a new mound, click "Record new mound". Your mounds IDs will automatically increment from the starting number in the "Next Mound ID" field which you can set manually.

  4. Mound: fill in fields as available. Red fields are required.

  5. Long press on the (little pencil) icon to bring up the annotation tool. Use the Annotation (little pencil) icon to enter in data or questions about fields that are unclear.

  6. Press the little info button for instructions about the field.

  7. Collect a GPS point while at the mound by clicking the "Take from GPS" button

  8. Take a picture by pressing the "Image button" from a distance, capturing the full mound outline, ideally with a scale.

  9. Navigate the module by using the sidebar (slide from the left edge to bring it up)

  10. All fields are auto-saved in 5 seconds after entry.

  11. Search for records by their ID or name. You can only see the records you have created.

  12. Send data to the server once you connect the device to wifi and activate the syncing (three dots menu).

Three dots menu in top right corner of the app has the module controls, such as GPS and syncing. You can set the GPS to internal or external (bluetooth) sensor. The location on your device (hardware GPS) must be switched on for the GPS in the app to work. If both are active the GPS indicator in the app turns blue.

Syncing backs up the data from the device to an online server. This process can take time and bandwidth. Syncing is off by default and should be switched off while in the field to save your battery. Activate it when back online. The wheel icon (most right) turns blue when the device is syncing, stays grey when inactive and goes red, if syncing is blocked/unavailable.

How do I get my data out?

You need to sync your data in order to get it out in a neat format. After you sync (the syncing wheel is blue and turns grey), please email enquiries@fedarch.org from the email account that you signed in to the module with and put "Community Mounds Data" in the subject line. You will receive an archive with data you recorded to date that has successfully been backed up online.

Need more data? Do you have a team? Want to send feedback? Please email enquiries@fedarch.org for more information on our data management services.
